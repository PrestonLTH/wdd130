Products

When it comes to having the best experience possible on a dirt bike, there are some accessories you can add to your bike to help make things go better. A company called Fastway by ProMoto Billet provides a plethora of accessories to help increase your experience with dirt biking. One product I would highly recommend you look into would be a kickstand. The fastway kickstand allows you to park your bike anywhere you stop without having to worry about it falling. And because of its internally sprung design, you will never have to worry about the leg falling down while you ride down the trail or catch some air off a jump.

The next product I would recommend looking into would be a set of footpegs. Yes your dirt bike should already come with footpegs, however, these footpegs will make your riding experience that much better. Because of their large platform design, it allows your foot to have more contact on the peg, therefore decreasing the chances of your foot sliding off and causing serious injury. And if you want to take it one step further, Fastway offers a patented design called the EXT which has an extension off the back of the peg that prevents your ankle from rolling backwards off the peg.
